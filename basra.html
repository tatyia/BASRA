BASRA

The Egyptian fishing card game.
First to 150 wins.

  • Play a card. It sweeps floor cards of the same rank — and any groups of number cards summing to its value.
  • J vacuums the whole floor (never a Basra).

HOW TO PLAY

Your turn: drag a card from your hand onto the floor (or just tap it). If it can capture, it does so automatically and the cards join your pile. Otherwise it stays on the floor.

  • Numbers (A–10): capture same rank + every group of number cards adding up to the card's value. Aces count as 1.
  • Q / K: capture only cards of the same rank.
  • J: captures the entire floor. Never scores a Basra.
  • 7♦ (Komi): captures the entire floor. Scores a Basra only if the floor is all number cards totaling ≤ 10.
  • Basra (+10): clearing the floor with any card except a Jack.
  • Round end: leftover floor cards go to whoever captured last. Majority of cards = +30 (tie: nobody).
  • Match: rounds repeat until someone reaches 150.
